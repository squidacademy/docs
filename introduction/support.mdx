## How to report a problem (best practice)

Please include:
1. **Page or Area** – URL or feature name (e.g., Classes → Assignments).
2. **What happened** – the error, unexpected behavior, or missing option.
3. **What you expected** – what you tried to do.
4. **Steps to reproduce** – numbered steps help a ton.
5. **Screenshots or short video** – if possible.

Send to **support@squid.gg**.
