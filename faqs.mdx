## Accounts & Access
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who creates my account?">
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Org Admins</strong> are created by a <strong>Partner</strong> or <strong>Reseller</strong>.</li>
      <li><strong>Teachers/Students</strong> are created by the <strong>Org Admin</strong> (your school).</li>
    </ul>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I forgot my password—what do I do?">
    Use <strong>Forgot Password</strong> on the sign-in page. If you don’t receive an email, ask your Org Admin to reset it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I can’t see my organization or classes.">
    You may not be assigned yet. Ask your Org Admin to add you to the correct org/class; then log out and back in.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my email?">
    Ask your Org Admin (or your Partner/Reseller if you’re an org admin). They can update user details in their console.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## LMS (Classes, Courses, Progress)
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do we create classes and add people?">
    Org Admins: go to <strong>Classes → Add Class</strong>, assign a teacher, pick modules/courses, then add students.
    See <a href="/user-guides/org-admins">Org Admins</a>.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can teachers add students to a class?">
    If enabled by your school, teachers can add students from the class <strong>Edit</strong> page. Otherwise, ask the Org Admin.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do we control lesson pacing?">
    Enable/disable chapters in the class <strong>Edit</strong> page (teachers or admins, depending on your school’s settings).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="A student finished a lesson but it didn’t “complete.”">
    Have them reopen the lesson and click <strong>Finish/Complete</strong>, then refresh. Teachers/Admins can verify progress on the class page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can we change course content?">
    You can choose which modules/chapters are active for each class. Course material itself is managed centrally.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tournaments
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I create a team?">
    Team Captains: open the tournament area, <strong>Create Team</strong>, set the name/logo, and invite players.
    See <a href="/user-guides/team-captains">Team Captains</a>.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do we register for an event?">
    Open the event page and click <strong>Register Team</strong>. Ensure your roster meets event requirements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who reports scores?">
    Captains report scores on the match page and attach proof if requested. Tournament Admins verify and resolve disputes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="A wrong score was reported—what now?">
    Contact a Tournament Admin immediately with evidence (screenshots, VOD). They will adjust results per the rules.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Partners & Resellers
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="We’re a Partner—how do we onboard schools or Resellers?">
    Use the Partner console to create organizations and (optionally) appoint Resellers.
